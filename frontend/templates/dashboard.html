Car Maintenance 2021 Mazda 3

Getting Started

  1. Update your odometer — click "Update Odometer" and enter your current km reading. Do this each time you fill up or visit the mechanic.
  2. Check your tasks — tasks are colour-coded: red means overdue or never done, amber means due soon, green means you're all good.
  3. Mark tasks done — after any service, click "Mark as Done", enter the km and date, and it will be saved to your maintenance log automatically.
